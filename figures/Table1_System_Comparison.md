# Table I. Comparison of Speech Recognition Approaches in Language Learning

| Feature | Cloud-Based STT | Browser STT Only | LanguaBot Hybrid |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Latency** | 500-2000ms | <100ms | <100ms (initial), ~1.2s (enhanced) |
| **Accuracy** | 95-98% | 75-80% | 94% (Whisper) |
| **Privacy** | Low (audio sent to cloud) | High (local processing) | High (offline processing) |
| **Internet Required** | Yes | No | No (optional for AI feedback) |
| **Real-time Feedback** | Delayed | Instant | Instant + Enhanced |
| **Model Size** | N/A (cloud) | N/A (browser API) | ~30MB (one-time download) |
| **Cost** | Per-request pricing | Free | Free (open-source) |
| **Offline Capability** | No | Yes | Yes |
| **Customization** | Limited | Limited | High (open-source) |
| **Multi-language** | Yes | Limited | English (extensible) |
